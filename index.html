Where It All Began

May 15, 2015

Ten years ago, Allyson sent us an application that would change the trajectory of our organization. Her words revealed not just her skills, but her heart for serving nonprofits:

"My journey to the nonprofit sector started when I began feeling that my marketing curriculum had a materialistic focus, and I craved something more. I found Wired Impact when researching media tactics for a nonprofit campaign supporting Take Back the Night, a march against interpersonal violence, in Lexington. I'm not a counselor, but I realized I could give to nonprofits with the skills I have in strategic planning, budgeting and execution.

I am a recent graduate from the University of Kentucky, and I am searching for a job where I can interact with people--better yet, passionate advocates! I am my best self when I can invest in others. In freshmen as a Resident Advisor on campus or in survivors of violence as they heal--I have found that I can easily distinguish what a person needs to feel supported. I will bring this enthusiasm and impactful thinking to Wired Impact.

On the side, I am fluent in sarcasm and have a healthy appreciation for donuts and large coffees!"
